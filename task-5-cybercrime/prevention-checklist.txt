 PREVENTION - CHECKLIST 
  
Stay Safe Online – Checklist 
• Never share your OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone  
• Do not accept unknown payment requests on UPI apps  
• Always verify the sender before making any transaction  
• Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages  
• Use only official and secure apps for payments  
• Enable two-factor authentication for accounts  
• Regularly check bank transaction history  
• Immediately report fraud on cybercrime.gov.in  
• Call helpline number 1930 in case of financial fraud
